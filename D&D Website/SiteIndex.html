The 5 best rules for DMing
  1. The game isn't all about you. It's the players who are playing.
  2. Let the dice do the talking.
  3. Make up everything.
  4. Don't forget about the passive traits.
  5. Your word is law. They need to deal with it.

In D&D, you can choose to be anyone! If you for some reason can't find a group to DM for or you are looking for other people to play with, have no fear! There are plenty of other people just like you. Head down to your LGS (Local Game Store) or check out an online forum ot two! Plenty of interesting people to meet everywhere you go! You would be surprised how many people play.
